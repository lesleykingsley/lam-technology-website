Networking & Infrastructure

Two lenses. One picture of who’s running your infrastructure.

Most environments aren’t run by one team. They’re run by a network of internal owners, MSPs, and ISPs — each with different scopes, different SLAs, and different bills. We use two lenses, side by side, to give you a single read on the whole picture.

Lens 1
Infrastructure Insight™
A read on the environment itself — the gear, the topology, the suppliers, and where risk is accumulating.
  • Network, data center, and connectivity health
  • Lifecycle, age, and end-of-support exposure
  • Architecture and segmentation review
  • Supplier diversity and concentration risk
  • Documented findings with prioritized recommendations
Lens 2
MSP Microscope™
A read on the relationship — what your MSP is contracted to do, what they’re actually delivering, and what it costs.
  • Contract scope vs. delivered services
  • Ticket and SLA performance
  • Spend benchmarking against market
  • Notice, exit, and renewal posture
  • Recommendation: stay, renegotiate, or transition
