Assessment Focus Areas

A comprehensive framework across every infrastructure domain.

Four structured focus areas cover the full scope of your environment, from baseline documentation to resilience readiness.

📂
Current Infrastructure Setup

Documents existing systems, platforms, and architecture. Identifies strengths, constraints, and areas of technical debt. Establishes a reliable baseline for future planning.

⚡
Performance & Resource Optimization

Identifies bottlenecks impacting availability, speed, and user experience. Evaluates alignment between infrastructure capacity and business demand.

Reviews infrastructure-level security posture. Identifies vulnerabilities, gaps, and risk exposure. Flags areas that may impact regulatory or audit readiness.
