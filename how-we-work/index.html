Supplier selection & direction.

Findings from the Insight phase directly inform your technology direction. With a clear picture of where you are, LAM helps you choose the right suppliers, negotiate the right contracts, and invest in the right priorities. Because LAM is supplier neutral, your strategy is built around your needs and budget — not our relationships. That independence is your competitive advantage.

SUPPLIER SHOOTOUT™

A structured, side-by-side evaluation of competing suppliers against your specific requirements. No guesswork, no salesperson spin.

SUPPLIER SELECTION & DIRECTION

Selection scoped and run end to end.

LAM scopes and runs the selection end to end — defining requirements, managing the evaluation, and helping you choose with expert objectivity.

CONTRACT NEGOTIATION

The leverage you don’t have alone.

Once a supplier is chosen, LAM negotiates the contract on your behalf — pricing, terms, SLAs, and exit clauses — with the leverage of market data and supplier relationships you don’t have alone.

03 — EXECUTION

Managed delivery & implementation.

When change is required, LAM manages the delivery from end to end. We act as your dedicated project manager, supplier liaison, and accountability partner throughout implementation. No finger-pointing between suppliers. No surprises on go-live day. Just disciplined execution.

DEDICATED DELIVERY™

Network, UCaaS, and CCaaS implementations.

Dedicated Delivery applies to network, UCaaS, and CCaaS implementations — LAM coordinates suppliers, timelines, and deliverables so your team stays focused on the business. For selected UCaaS suppliers, we are certified to install services directly as a paid engagement.

DEDICATED DISCONNECT™

End-of-life handled with the same rigor.

Managing the end-of-life of legacy services is just as important as standing up new ones. LAM handles disconnects, migrations, and cutover with the same rigor as deployments. Dedicated Disconnect™ is a paid engagement.

CONTRACT & SUPPLIER MANAGEMENT

Active oversight of contracts and supplier performance during the life of the engagement, so the deal you signed is the deal you get.
