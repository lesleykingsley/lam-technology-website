Why It Matters

The costs hiding in plain sight add up fast.

Most organizations are paying for things they don’t use, missing renewals that lock them in, and leaving negotiation leverage on the table — without realizing it.

Auto-renewals pass unnoticed and lock organizations into multi-year terms at above-market rates.

Renewal timing is everything — missing the window means another term at a price you didn’t choose.
