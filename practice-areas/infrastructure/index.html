THE QUESTION WE START WITH

Most environments aren’t run by one team. They’re run by a network of internal owners, MSPs, and ISPs — each with different scopes, different SLAs, and different bills. We use two lenses, side by side, to give you a single read on the whole picture.

Two Lenses, One Picture

Infrastructure Insight™

A read on the environment itself — the gear, the topology, the suppliers, and where risk is accumulating. Network, data center, and connectivity health. Lifecycle, age, and end-of-support exposure. Architecture and segmentation review. Supplier diversity and concentration risk. Documented findings with prioritized recommendations.

MSP Microscope™

A read on the relationship — what your MSP is contracted to do, what they’re actually delivering, and what it costs. Contract scope vs. delivered services. Ticket and SLA performance. Spend benchmarking against market. Notice, exit, and renewal posture. Recommendation: stay, renegotiate, or transition.
