Maturity Scoring Model

A scoring model designed to tell the truth.

Each of the 16 domains is scored independently on a 0–5 scale, then rolled up into an overall maturity picture. You only score a level if it works across the enterprise—not in pockets.

ScoreLevelMeaningReality Check
0UndefinedNo consistent processHeroics and tribal knowledge
1Ad HocReactive, inconsistentWorks until it doesn't
2RepeatableBasic consistencyStill people-dependent
3DefinedDocumented, measuredPredictable outcomes
4ManagedProactively governedPerformance is steered
5OptimizedAutomated, scalableBusiness-aligned by design
Red Flag Rule: Any domain scoring ≤2 represents a material risk—regardless of your average score. These must be addressed before committing to an MSP model.
