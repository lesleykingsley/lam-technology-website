MSP MICROSCOPE™ SELF-CHECK

Your MSP relationship is probably costing more than it should. Find out in five minutes.

Ten questions about your current MSP arrangement — what you outsource, what you pay, and how much visibility you actually have into the work being done. At the end, a plain-language report names where the friction or leakage likely is, and what the full MSP Microscope™ engagement would dig into next.

This is not a tool for changing your MSP. We’re not going to tell you to fire anyone based on a 10-question survey, and we don’t replace MSPs ourselves.

What this is: a fast read on whether the relationship you have is the one you should keep, restructure, or renegotiate. The Self-Check surfaces the signals. The full MSP Microscope™ engagement is what validates them, prices the alternatives, and runs the change if one is warranted.

A typical mid-market MSP relationship has 10–20% of spend that’s recoverable, restructurable, or worth renegotiating. On a sloppy one, 25%+. The best time to look is 6–9 months before contract end. Inside 3–4 months, there isn’t enough runway to make a change.
