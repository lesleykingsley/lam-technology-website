The Process

Insight. Strategy. Execution. Support.

No shortcuts, no gaps, and no vendor agenda. Just a clear process that gets you to the right outcome.

01 / Insight

Assessments & Discovery

Before we recommend anything, we need to understand where you actually are. Most organizations are surprised by what we find in the Insight phase.

Our branded assessments are paid engagements that deliver a clear, objective picture of your environment before you commit to any direction, vendor, or investment. Findings from this phase directly inform everything that follows.

Important

All Insight phase assessments are paid engagements. This ensures our team is fully committed to your engagement and that findings are thorough, documented, and actionable.

Billing Breakthrough™ →

A comprehensive review of your telecom and IT invoices that uncovers billing errors, auto-renewed contracts, and renegotiation opportunities across your service portfolio.

Step 1 for Most Clients
Infrastructure Insight™ →

A deep assessment of your network, cloud, and IT infrastructure to identify gaps, redundancies, and modernization opportunities.

Vigilance Verification™ →

A comprehensive cybersecurity risk assessment that identifies vulnerabilities, evaluates your current posture, and prioritizes remediation.

Experience Examination™ →

An evaluation of your customer experience infrastructure and digital engagement tools to uncover friction, gaps, and improvement opportunities.

Support Scope™ →

A structured review of your existing IT support model, managed services contracts, and vendor relationships to right-size coverage and cost.

Wireless Wisdom™

Supplier Shootout™

A structured, side-by-side evaluation of competing vendors against your specific requirements. No guesswork, no salesperson spin.

RFP Vendor Validation™

LAM builds and manages your RFP process end to end, ensuring you ask the right questions and evaluate responses with expert objectivity.

02 / Strategy

Supplier Selection & Vendor Strategy

Findings from the Insight phase directly inform your technology direction. With a clear picture of where you are, LAM helps you choose the right vendors, negotiate the right contracts, and invest in the right priorities.

Because LAM has no vendor loyalty, your strategy is built around your needs and budget, not our relationships. That independence is your competitive advantage.

03 / Execution

Managed Delivery & Implementation

When change is required, LAM manages the delivery from end to end. We act as your dedicated project manager, vendor liaison, and accountability partner throughout implementation.

No finger-pointing between vendors. No surprises on go-live day. Just disciplined execution with full accountability on our side.

Dedicated Delivery™

LAM takes ownership of technology implementations, coordinating vendors, timelines, and deliverables so your team stays focused on the business.

Dedicated Disconnect™

Managing the end-of-life of legacy services is just as important as standing up new ones. LAM handles disconnects, migrations, and cutover with the same rigor as deployments.

Contract & Vendor Management

Ongoing oversight of your vendor contracts, renewals, and performance to ensure you never overpay or get locked into terms that no longer serve you.

Renewal Advocacy

When contracts come up for renewal, LAM negotiates on your behalf with the leverage of deep vendor relationships and market knowledge you don't have alone.

04 / Support

Ongoing Optimization & Advocacy

The engagement doesn't end at go-live. LAM stays alongside your organization to protect the value of every technology investment over time.

From managing renewals to escalating vendor issues to monitoring performance, LAM is your long-term advocate, not just a one-time consultant.
