The Process

Insight. Strategy. Execution. Support.

No shortcuts, no gaps, and no supplier agenda. Just a clear process that gets you to the right outcome.

01 / Insight

Start with what you actually have.

Before LAM recommends any direction, we get the facts on the table. Every engagement starts with one of six branded assessments — the right lens for the question you're trying to answer.

Where Most Start
IT Spend & Contracts
Billing Breakthrough™ & Contract Clarifier™
A one-time snapshot of every IT service you're paying for, by supplier and location, with renewal dates and notice windows mapped.
Cybersecurity & Risk
Vigilance Verification™
Validate where your security posture actually stands — against the threats your business faces, not a generic checklist.
Networking & Infrastructure
Infrastructure Insight™
A clear read on the network, connectivity, and infrastructure your business runs on — and where it's quietly holding you back.
Customer Experience
Experience Examination™
How customers actually experience your business across voice, digital, and self-service — and what's worth fixing first.
Mobility & IoT
Wireless Wisdom™
A practical evaluation of your mobility, wireless, and IoT footprint — cost, coverage, and lifecycle in one picture.
AI & Strategy
AI Readiness Radar™
An honest read on whether your data, processes, and people are positioned for AI — and where to invest first.
Supplier Shootout™

A structured, side-by-side evaluation of competing suppliers against your specific requirements. No guesswork, no salesperson spin.

Supplier Selection & Direction

LAM scopes and runs the selection end to end — defining requirements, managing the evaluation, and helping you choose with expert objectivity.

Contract Negotiation

Once a supplier is chosen, LAM negotiates the contract on your behalf — pricing, terms, SLAs, and exit clauses — with the leverage of market data and supplier relationships you don't have alone.

02 / Strategy

Supplier Selection & Direction

Findings from the Insight phase directly inform your technology direction. With a clear picture of where you are, LAM helps you choose the right suppliers, negotiate the right contracts, and invest in the right priorities.

Because LAM is supplier neutral, your strategy is built around your needs and budget — not our relationships. That independence is your competitive advantage.

03 / Execution

Managed Delivery & Implementation

When change is required, LAM manages the delivery from end to end. We act as your dedicated project manager, supplier liaison, and accountability partner throughout implementation.

No finger-pointing between suppliers. No surprises on go-live day. Just disciplined execution.

Dedicated Delivery™

Dedicated Delivery applies to network, UCaaS, and CCaaS implementations — LAM coordinates suppliers, timelines, and deliverables so your team stays focused on the business. For selected UCaaS suppliers, we are certified to install services directly as a paid engagement.

Dedicated Disconnect™

Managing the end-of-life of legacy services is just as important as standing up new ones. LAM handles disconnects, migrations, and cutover with the same rigor as deployments.

Contract & Supplier Management

Active oversight of contracts and supplier performance during the life of the engagement, so the deal you signed is the deal you get.

Ongoing Optimization

Joint business reviews with your key suppliers, run alongside your team — making sure performance, spend, and roadmap stay aligned to your business as it changes.

Renewal Advocacy

When contracts come up for renewal, LAM negotiates on your behalf with the leverage of deep supplier relationships and market knowledge you don't have alone.

04 / Support

Ongoing Optimization & Advocacy

The engagement doesn't end at go-live. LAM stays alongside your organization to protect the value of every technology investment over time.

From managing renewals to escalating supplier issues to monitoring performance, LAM is your long-term advocate — not just a one-time consultant.
